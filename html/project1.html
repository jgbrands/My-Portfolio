Raycaster Engine

A simple raycasting engine based on the game Wolfenstein3D, made using C/C++ with OpenGL. The raycasting algorithm consists of drawing lines from the position of the player towards the game world to find the distances from objects, then uses that distance to determine the heights of each column of pixels that will be drawn to the screen.
